Checklist for updating SteelIndustries after a release:

- Check if the version has been updated in Reference
- Check if the version has been updated in MCMOD.info
- Check if the version has been updated in build.gradle
- Major release? (new features, major stuff) new number (0.5 > 0.6)
- Minor release? (bugfixes) new letter ( 0.5a > 0.5b)
